The truth of Me

About Myself, I'll gladly share, In verses woven with utmost care. A tapestry of life, both bright and deep, In these lines, my secrets I will keep. I am a soul, a spirit free, A traveler on life's vast, grand sea. With dreams that reach for skies above, And a heart that's filled with boundless love. I'm a puzzle, a riddle, a mystery's art, With layers of feelings that fill my heart. I've laughter that dances like morning's ray, And tears that fall when skies turn gray. In my eyes, you'll find the stories I've read, The places I've been, the tears I've shed. In every smile, a memory stored, Of moments cherished, of bonds adored. I'm a book of pages, some worn, some new, Each chapter revealing a different hue. I'm a melody of laughter and song, A canvas of emotions, where I belong. I'm a whisper of hope, a scream of despair, A heart full of courage, strong and rare. I'm the sum of my choices, the paths I've trod, A symphony of life, created by God. So, in this verse, I lay myself bare, A glimpse of the soul beneath the wear. In every word, a piece of me, For the world to know, for all to see.
